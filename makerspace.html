Welcome to the Makerspace.

The Makerspace is where designers, engineers, and makers can explore open source plans for medical supplies currently needed by those on the frontlines of the COVID19 pandemic. In addition, the makerspace is intended as a platform for collaboration on currently incomplete or unapproved open source designs. Through the makerspace progress can be tracked on completion and approval.
