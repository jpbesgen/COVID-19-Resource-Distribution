About Us

We are a team of UC Berkely students who hope to create and mobilize a community of makers, manufacturers, and designers to help cover the increasingly large gaps within the medical supply chain in the United States. Currently, many Americans are stuck at home watching as household after household falls to the tragedy of the COVID-19 pandemic. We hope to transform that feeling of helplessness into the same energy that has pulled America through every crisis from the Great Depression to WW2. As our grandparents collected cans for bullets and rolled bandages for first aid kits we hope that we will see individual Americans and small businesses mobilize to open source the designs for vital medical equipment, sew surgical masks, construct gowns, build face shields, and 3D print much-needed materials for the struggle we now face against COVID-19.
